Title: Night of the Last Light

Chapter 1

The city was silent, except for the occasional distant scream. Emma clutched her backpack, heart pounding as she ran down the cracked streets. The sun had set hours ago, and darkness wrapped the city like a thick, suffocating blanket.

Behind her, low growls echoed from the shadows. She knew what was coming—creatures that once were human, now hungry, relentless zombies. She darted into an alley, knocking over a trash can to slow them down.

Her safehouse was just a block away. She sprinted faster, tripping over rubble, and barely regained her footing. The moans grew louder, closer, and she could feel hot, foul breath behind her.
